Realistic Gallery

What is a realistic tattoo?

People - popstars, footballers, your spouse or child; animals - wild or domesticated; the subject can be whatever you imagine, I just need a photograph that I can reproduce for you. Maybe you can get inspiration here!

New-School Gallery

What is New-School?

New-school is a tattooing style characterised by the use of heavy outlines, vivid colours and exaggerated depictions of the subject. In this gallery I have included some tattoos which may not be strictly new-school but certainly are not traditional.
